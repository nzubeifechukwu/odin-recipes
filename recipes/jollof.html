Jollof Rice

Description

To make decilious Nigerian jollof, you need the following ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Chicken or beef
  • etc.

Steps

Follow these steps to make jollof rice:

  1. Boil the rice until it's half-done
  2. Wash the rice
  3. Heat the vegetable oil a little
  4. Add onions and tomato paste
  5. etc.
